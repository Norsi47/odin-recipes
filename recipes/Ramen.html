Making Ramen

Description

Hello this is just some basic stuff about how to make Ramen, nice dish I enjoy eating once in a while but it causes a lot of acne on my skin for some reason.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • The Ramen itself
  • The ingredients inside the packet
  • Any outside ingriedient you feel like adding, pepper for example
  • Any outside protein, for example eggs or fish
  • Thats it

Steps

  1. Boil Water and do not put Ramen in the pot yet until it looks boiled, or just dump it in
  2. Add Ramen into pot
  3. Stir it a little with a spoon while it is in the pot
  4. Add the spices that came from the packet and also any other outside spice
  5. Cook the protein needed, for example boiled eggs or chicken
  6. Make sure your Ramen is cooked and take it out
  7. Add the Protein inside the Ramen when done
  8. Enjoy!
