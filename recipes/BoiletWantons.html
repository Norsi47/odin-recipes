Making Wontons

Description

Boiled Wontons is something I started getting into because of Arleen Joy the one and only nice quick meal to eat to keep you full and ready for a couple of hours.

Ingredients

  • Water in a Pot
  • The Wontons themselves
  • Any Sauce you would like to dip the Wontons in

Steps

  1. Boil Water in the Pot
  2. Add the Amount you would like to put in, into the pot
  3. Keep Checking the Wontons from time to time
  4. Remove if Wontons have started to chance color and the inside looks cooked
  5. Enjoy this quick and great meal
